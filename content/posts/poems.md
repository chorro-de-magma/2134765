people who very clearly don't see themself very clearly. their private life degrades into performance. ther

material elites vs moral elites  
of course they're winning  
from their sinning  
  
the sink sunk from the drying rack flood

mediated by responses to the reviews

stark original batch felt like a brail autopsy

catch my tail in the darkness of the copies

green rubble, grey shoots

can't tell the taste on your tongue

cold wind can't smell where it's from 

they are 

for them it was nothing

anger spreaded by proxy

halucinatory doxing

feathers ruffling

divided like a domino  
pass onto the next

hereditory noise 

economic leakage
